# HR Workforce Diversity & Attrition Dashboard

## Project Overview

This project focuses on analyzing workforce diversity, employee attrition, and employee engagement metrics using Tableau. The dashboard helps HR teams identify attrition risks, monitor workforce diversity, evaluate employee satisfaction, and improve retention strategies.

---

## Objective

The objective of this project is to:

* Monitor workforce diversity across departments
* Analyze employee attrition patterns
* Track employee satisfaction and work-life balance
* Identify high-risk employee groups
* Support data-driven HR decision-making

---

## Tools Used

* Tableau Public
* Microsoft Excel
* HR Analytics
* Business Intelligence
* Data Visualization

---

## Dataset Information

The dataset includes:

* Employee ID
* Employee Name
* Age
* Gender
* Department
* Job Role
* Location
* Education Level
* Years at Company
* Monthly Salary
* Performance Rating
* Job Satisfaction Score
* Work-Life Balance Score
* Training Hours
* Promotion Status
* Overtime Status
* Employment Type
* Diversity Category
* Attrition Status
* Attrition Risk Level

---

## Dashboard KPIs

* Total Employees
* Attrition Count
* Average Salary
* Employee Satisfaction Score
* Work-Life Balance Score

---

## Visualizations

* Attrition by Department
* Gender Distribution
* Attrition Risk Analysis
* Salary vs Satisfaction Scatter Plot
* Employment Type Distribution
* KPI Cards

---

## Key Insights

* Identified departments with higher attrition rates
* Analyzed workforce diversity distribution
* Evaluated employee satisfaction and work-life balance
* Identified high-risk employee groups
* Supported workforce planning and retention initiatives
